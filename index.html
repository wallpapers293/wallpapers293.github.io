How to Apply Wallpaper

For Android:

  • Download the wallpaper using the "Download" button.
  • Open your phone's Gallery or Photos app.
  • Select the downloaded wallpaper.
  • Tap the options menu and select "Set as Wallpaper."

For iOS:

  • Download the wallpaper using the "Download" button.
  • Open the Photos app and select the downloaded wallpaper.
  • Tap the share icon (box with an arrow) and select "Use as Wallpaper."
  • Adjust the position and tap "Set" to apply.
